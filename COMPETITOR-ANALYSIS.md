# Competitive Analysis: DOB Alerts vs ViolationAlert

> Deep analysis completed 2026-05-13

## Executive Summary

DOB Alerts is the incumbent (since 2011), running on ASP.NET (.aspx pages). They only cover DOB/311 complaints with hourly scanning. **3 newer competitors** are eating their lunch:

| Feature | DOB Alerts | ViolationWatch | DOBGuard | **ViolationAlert (us)** |
|---------|-----------|----------------|----------|------------------------|
| Agencies | DOB/311 only | 8+ agencies | 6 agencies | **10+ agencies** |
| Detection | Hourly | 10-20 min | Hours | **Daily (upgradeable)** |
| Pricing | $19/mo (1 prop) | $9.99/mo | $49.99/10 props | **Free / $29 Pro** |
| Free trial | No | Yes | Free lookup | **Free tier (3 props)** |
| Resolution help | None | None | AI risk scoring | **AI KB + contractor marketplace** |
| Tech stack | ASP.NET (.aspx) | Modern | Modern | **Next.js 16** |

## Their Pricing
- **Starter**: $19/mo for 1 property
- **Save-As-You-Grow**: Tiered, ~$1.50/property at scale
- **Premium**: Up to 500 properties at $750/mo
- No free trial, no free tier

## Their Messaging
- "Avoid headaches. Get heads-up alerts."
- "Monitor my jobsites 24/7"
- "NYC's trusted complaint-alert service since 2011"
- "It's all alerts. Only alerts. All the time."

## Their Strengths
1. 15 years in market — trust through longevity
2. Cheapest at scale ($1.50/property)
3. Simple, focused product
4. Established customer base in construction/real estate

## Their Critical Weaknesses
1. **Single agency** — only DOB/311, no HPD/ECB/FDNY/DSNY/DEP
2. **Hourly scanning** — competitors do 10-20 min
3. **No free trial** — straight to paid
4. **Ancient tech** — ASP.NET, likely poor mobile
5. **Zero thought leadership** — no blog, no educational content
6. **No social proof** — zero testimonials, case studies, logos
7. **No intelligent filtering** — raw alerts, no risk scoring
8. **No resolution workflow** — just alerts, leaves you hanging
9. **No integrations** — no API, Slack, Zapier
10. **No enterprise features** — no teams, audit trails

## Our Moat (What Nobody Else Has)
1. **Resolution Engine** — AI knowledge base that gets smarter over time
2. **Contractor Marketplace** — "now what?" moment monetized
3. **CRM** — building management, not just alerts
4. **10+ agencies** — most comprehensive coverage
5. **Free tier** — 3 properties free forever
